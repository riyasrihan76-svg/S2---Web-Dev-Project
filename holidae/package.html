Package Overview

Experience the magic of Bali — the Island of the Gods. This 7-night package takes you through lush rice terraces, ancient Hindu temples, vibrant markets, and some of the world's most stunning beaches. Whether you're after relaxation, adventure, or culture, Bali has it all.

Your stay includes a hand-picked 4-star resort in Seminyak, daily breakfast, and a guided island tour. Flights from major UK airports are included in the base price.

Itinerary Highlights

Day 1
Arrival & Check-In

Transfer to your resort. Spend the evening exploring Seminyak's famous beach clubs and restaurants.
